Gatekeeper Settings

Captcha-based verification system for new members joining the server.

Gatekeeper automatically assigns a role to a member when they join, for the sole purpose of blocking them from accessing the server. The user must press a button to verify themselves and have that role removed.

To set up gatekeeper, a few things are required:

  • A channel that locked users will see but regular users will not.
  • A role that is assigned when users join.
  • A message that the bot sends in the channel with the verify button.

Optional settings:

  • A role that is assigned when users finish verification (Starter Role).

There are also settings to help configure some aspects of it:

  • Auto (Rate Limit) automatically triggers the gatekeeper if N members join within a span of M seconds.
  • Bypass Action configures what action is taken when a user talks or joins voice before verifying.

Note that once gatekeeper is enabled — even automatically — it must be manually disabled.

ⓘ Users verify by solving an image captcha consisting of 6 random letters they need to type into the chat.
