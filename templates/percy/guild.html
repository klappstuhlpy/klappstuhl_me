Entrance Protection

Captcha-gated verification that holds new members until they prove they're human.

How it works

When a member joins, they're immediately assigned a lockdown role that hides every channel except a designated verification channel. Inside that channel a captcha button awaits — solving it removes the role and grants full access.

Prerequisites

  1. Verification Channel — visible only to held members.
  2. Lockdown Role — strips channel access on assignment.
  3. Captcha Message — the embed with the solve button, deployed to the channel.

Enforcement

If a held member attempts to speak or join voice before verifying, the configured bypass action (kick or ban) fires immediately. The auto-trigger threshold activates sentinel automatically when join velocity spikes — once engaged, only a moderator can stand it down.

ⓘ Captchas are 6-character alphanumeric images. Members type their answer directly in chat within 90 seconds.
